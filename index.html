📏 Size Guide

How to measure yourself for a perfect fit blouse

🫁
Bust / Chest
Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.
e.g. 36"
🤸
Waist
Measure around the narrowest part of your natural waistline.
e.g. 30"
📐
Blouse Length
From shoulder tip down to where you want the blouse to end (usually 13–16 inches).
e.g. 15"
💪
Arm Hole / Sleeve
Measure around the upper arm where the sleeve begins. Relax your arm by your side.
e.g. 14"
🔙
Back Neck to Waist
From the back of your neck (base) straight down to your natural waistline.
e.g. 14"

💡 Pro Tips
✔ Measure over innerwear, not over a blouse.
✔ Don't pull the tape too tight — keep it snug but comfortable.
✔ Ask someone to help for more accuracy.
✔ We add 0.5" ease automatically for comfort.
